Healthcare Fabrics

Healthcare Fabrics

We specialize in the production of coated technical textiles for the use of mattress, pillow and wheelchair cushion protectors with wide array of Polyutrehane and PVC fabrics specially for hospital and nursing home applications. Suitable for use in both medical and commercial settings, our polyurethane coated fabric provides a fluid-proof barrier for infection control that is launderable at 95°c and waterproof.  In our technical textile division, we produce PU and PVC coated fabrics with flame retardant, antibacterial & antifungal, water impermeable, machine washable at 95 °C, breathable and bielastic characteristics in different widths of 150, 160, up to 220 cm. On top of being able to deliver fabric in rolls, Vinteks is also specialized in high end confectioned goods and seamless high frequency welded products for mattress protection and bed accessories.
